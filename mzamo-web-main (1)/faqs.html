Frequently asked questions

Question 1: Where can I buy branded school uniform?
You can buy uniform at lowveld stores or order it online through the school website
Question 2: What happens if my child has disability or any allergens?
Report to the disability first aid office, letting them know of the precautions they suppose to follow
Question 3: How does the school communicate with parents and involve them in the life of the school?
Mostly the school communicates with letters for school meetings, but incase of emergency, the head of the school will contact the guardian directly
Question 4: Any activities that are implemented in the school that helps children to be fit, health, strong, and confident?
Fortunately, we have sport activities which includes basket ball, netball, and soccer for fitness, we also have debate, speech, and reading competitions to boost children's confident, reading, and writing skills.
Question 5: What do I need to meet or contact a teacher?
Make an appointment with the teacher through admin office or through your child.
